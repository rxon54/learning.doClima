# CHIRPS — Climate Hazards InfraRed Precipitation with Stations

Gridded precipitation product from UC Santa Barbara's Climate Hazards Center. Blends satellite infrared estimates with ground station data. Resolution: 0.05° (~5.5 km). Coverage: 50°S–50°N, 1981–present. Updated monthly (with ~3-week lag) and daily.

**Why it matters for doClima:** Primary precipitation data source for historical indicators and trend analysis at municipal level.

**Key limitation:** Inherits station network biases — coverage is weaker in the Amazon and Semiárido where stations are sparse.
