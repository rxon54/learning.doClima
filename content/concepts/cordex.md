# CORDEX — Coordinated Regional Climate Downscaling Experiment

International framework for coordinated regional climate downscaling. The South America domain (CORDEX-SA) provides finer-resolution climate projections for the continent using multiple regional climate models driven by CMIP5/6 GCMs.

**Why it matters for doClima:** AdaptaBrasil projections likely use CORDEX-SA or equivalent downscaled data. Explains the jump from 100km GCM to ~25km regional projections used in Brazilian datasets.
