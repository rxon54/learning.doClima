# DEC / ECP — Decreto de Emergência / Estado de Calamidade Pública

Brazil's two-tier disaster declaration system. DEC (Decreto de Emergência): situation manageable with existing resources — unlocks partial federal aid. ECP (Estado de Calamidade Pública): resources overwhelmed — unlocks full federal aid and special budget rules.

**Key bias:** Political incentive to declare emergencies creates over-reporting. Some municipalities are "perennially in emergency." Data quality is uneven — improved significantly post-2012.
