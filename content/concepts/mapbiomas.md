# MapBiomas — Annual Land Use and Cover Maps for Brazil

Annual land use and land cover maps for Brazil at 30m resolution, 1985–present (Collection 9). Produced by a multi-institutional network using Landsat imagery and machine learning. ~30 land use classes.

**Reliability hierarchy:** native forest (very high) > pasture (high) > agriculture (moderate) > secondary vegetation (lower) > urban (moderate)

**Key distinction:** gross deforestation (total area cleared) vs net deforestation (cleared minus regrowth). Policy discussions often conflate these.

**Your DB:** `mapbiomas.db` (~294 MB)
