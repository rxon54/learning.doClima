# ERA5 — ECMWF Reanalysis v5

Gold-standard global atmospheric reanalysis from ECMWF (European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts). Covers 1940–present at ~31 km resolution. Assimilates observations + satellite data into a physics-based model. Provides temperature, wind, humidity, precipitation, and 200+ other variables.

**Why it matters for doClima:** Best source for historical temperature variables where station data is sparse. Preferred for WBGT and heat stress calculations.

**Key caveat:** Not pure observations — it's a model output constrained by observations. Biases exist, especially for precipitation in complex terrain.
