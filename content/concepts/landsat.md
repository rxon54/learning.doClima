# Landsat — Long-Term Land Observation Satellites

NASA/USGS satellite program running since 1972. Current: Landsat 8 and 9. Resolution: 30m multispectral. Revisit: every 16 days. The backbone of global land use change analysis — its 50-year archive is what makes MapBiomas Collection 9 (1985-2023) possible.
