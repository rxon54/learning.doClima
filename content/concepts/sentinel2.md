# Sentinel-2 — ESA High-Resolution Land Monitoring

ESA (European Space Agency) satellite constellation. 10m resolution multispectral. Revisit: every 5 days (with both satellites). Operational since 2015. Free and open data.

**Advantage over Landsat:** 3x higher resolution and 3x more frequent revisit. Better for detecting small agricultural fields, urban expansion, and recent land use changes.

**Limitation:** no historical archive before 2015, so can't replace Landsat for trend analysis going back to the 1980s.
