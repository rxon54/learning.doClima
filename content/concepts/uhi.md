# UHI — Urban Heat Island

Cities are typically 3-8°C warmer than surrounding rural areas due to reduced vegetation, increased impervious surfaces, waste heat from buildings/vehicles, and urban canyon geometry trapping longwave radiation.

**Implications for doClima:** CHIRTS/ERA5 gridded temperature data reflects the broader landscape, potentially underestimating peak temperatures in dense urban cores. Heat vulnerability indicators for state capitals need UHI adjustment.
