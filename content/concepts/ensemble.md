# Ensemble — Multi-Model Climate Uncertainty

Running multiple climate models on the same scenario to quantify uncertainty. The spread between models = structural uncertainty (different physical parameterizations). AdaptaBrasil likely uses ensemble means or specific percentiles (e.g. median, 10th/90th percentile).

**Reading ensemble output:** if all models agree → high confidence. If they spread widely → low confidence, use range not single value.
