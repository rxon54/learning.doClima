# PNACC — Plano Nacional de Adaptação à Mudança do Clima

Brazil's national climate adaptation plan, published by MCTI. Defines 9 thematic axes (eixos temáticos) with specific adaptation objectives covering: water resources, food security, urban areas, biodiversity, health, coastal zones, disaster risk, energy, and industry.

**Why it matters for doClima:** The PNACC objectives define what municipalities NEED — aligning doClima features to these axes makes the product legible to government partners and grant funders.
