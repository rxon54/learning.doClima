# TCFD — Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures

Framework created by the Financial Stability Board for companies to disclose climate-related financial risks. Two risk categories: **physical risks** (acute: floods, storms; chronic: sea level rise, heat stress) and **transition risks** (policy changes, stranded assets).

**Why it matters for doClima:** TCFD language reframes the product from "climate data tool" to "physical risk assessment for financial decision-making." Banks, agribusinesses, and insurers with Brazilian operations need exactly what doClima provides — municipal-level physical risk — to meet TCFD disclosure requirements.

TCFD merged into ISSB (International Sustainability Standards Board) in 2023; ISSB S2 is now the global baseline standard.
