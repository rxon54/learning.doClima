# IDF Curves — Intensity-Duration-Frequency

Engineering tool relating rainfall intensity (mm/h), duration (minutes to days), and return period (how rare the event is). A "1-hour, 100-year event" = rainfall intensity in 1 hour with 1% annual probability.

**Why it matters for doClima:** IDF curves underpin urban drainage design and flash flood risk assessment. Municipalities with outdated IDF curves (based on pre-1990 data) are systematically underestimating flood risk as climate shifts the frequency of extremes.
