# COBRADE — Codificação Brasileira de Desastres

Brazil's official disaster classification system. Two main branches: natural (climatic, geological, biological) and technological. Key climate categories: 1.1 drought, 1.2 flood (gradual), 1.3 flash flood (enxurrada), 1.4 landslide (deslizamento), 1.5 storm (tempestade), 2.1 epidemic.

**Why it matters for doClima:** S2ID/Atlas Digital records use COBRADE codes. You need this to query and interpret historical disaster data correctly.
