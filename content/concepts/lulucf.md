# LULUCF — Land Use, Land Use Change and Forestry

GHG accounting sector covering emissions and removals from forests, agriculture, and land management. In Brazil, LULUCF (dominated by Amazonian deforestation) is historically the largest emissions sector — far exceeding energy and industry combined.

**Why it matters for doClima:** SEEG municipal estimates are most reliable for LULUCF because deforestation can be directly measured from satellite data (MapBiomas, PRODES), unlike energy or agriculture which rely on proxies.
