# GCM — Global Climate Model

Physics-based simulation of the coupled atmosphere-ocean-land-ice system. Runs at ~100-200 km resolution. Roughly 40 GCMs from research centers worldwide contribute to CMIP6. They differ in how they parameterize clouds, convection, ocean mixing, and other sub-grid processes — this is why they disagree.

**Cannot predict weather** — models only reproduce the statistical properties of climate (averages, variability, extremes) over multi-decadal periods.
