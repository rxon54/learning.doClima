# SSP-RCP — Shared Socioeconomic Pathways + Representative Concentration Pathways

CMIP6's combined scenario framework pairing socioeconomic development paths (SSPs) with greenhouse gas concentration trajectories (RCPs).

Key pairs for Brazil:
- **SSP1-2.6:** strong mitigation, sustainable development. Best case.
- **SSP2-4.5:** middle-of-the-road, moderate mitigation. Most likely near-term.
- **SSP5-8.5:** fossil-fueled development, no mitigation. Worst case / high-end risk assessment.

AdaptaBrasil uses SSP2-4.5 (optimistic scenario) and SSP5-8.5 (pessimistic scenario).
