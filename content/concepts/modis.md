# MODIS — Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer

NASA sensor on Terra and Aqua satellites. 250m–1km resolution, daily coverage since 2000. Key products for doClima context: MOD13 (NDVI/EVI vegetation indices), MCD64 (burned area), MOD11 (land surface temperature).

**Why it matters:** daily coverage makes it ideal for near-real-time fire detection and vegetation monitoring. INPE's Queimadas fire monitoring system uses MODIS.
