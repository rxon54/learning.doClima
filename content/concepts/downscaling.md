# Downscaling — From Global to Local Climate Projections

Process of converting coarse GCM output (~100-200 km) to finer spatial resolution usable for regional/municipal analysis. Two main approaches:

- **Dynamical:** run a Regional Climate Model (RCM) nested inside the GCM. Higher physical fidelity, expensive. Brazil's Eta model (INPE) is this type.
- **Statistical:** use historical relationships between large-scale patterns and local observations. Cheaper, more common in national datasets.

**Why it matters for doClima:** AdaptaBrasil's projected indicators have been downscaled. Understanding this explains resolution limits and why "municipal-level" projections still carry uncertainty.
