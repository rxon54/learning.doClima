# SEEG — Sistema de Estimativas de Emissões e Remoções de GEE

Brazil's GHG emissions estimation system, published annually by Observatório do Clima (civil society coalition, not government). Covers all 5 IPCC sectors across all 5,570 municipalities.

**Critical caveat for municipal data:** only [[lulucf]] has direct municipal-level measurement (via deforestation mapping). Energy, industry, agriculture, and waste are ESTIMATED using proxies (population, GDP, livestock headcount from IBGE). Municipal-level precision is lower for non-LULUCF sectors.
