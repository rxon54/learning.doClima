# CMIP6 — Coupled Model Intercomparison Project Phase 6

Coordinated experiment where ~40 GCM groups worldwide run standardized climate scenarios. Feeds into IPCC AR6 reports. Combines SSP socioeconomic pathways with RCP radiative forcing levels into SSP-RCP pairs (e.g. SSP2-4.5, SSP5-8.5).

**Why it matters for doClima:** AdaptaBrasil's projected data (2030, 2040, 2050) is derived from CMIP6 models. Understanding this explains why projections have uncertainty ranges.
