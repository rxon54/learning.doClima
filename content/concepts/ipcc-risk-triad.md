# IPCC Risk Triad — Hazard × Exposure × Vulnerability

The foundational framework for climate risk assessment from IPCC AR5/AR6.

- **Hazard:** the physical event (flood, drought, heat wave, storm)
- **Exposure:** people, assets, or ecosystems in the path of the hazard
- **Vulnerability:** susceptibility to harm = Sensitivity × (lack of) Adaptive Capacity
- **Risk:** the combination of all three. Remove any leg and risk drops.

AR6 addition: the possibility of maladaptation (responses that backfire) is now part of the risk framing.
