# CHIRTS — Climate Hazards center InfraRed Temperature with Stations

CHIRPS equivalent for temperature. Maximum and minimum daily air temperature at 0.05° resolution, 1983–present. Produced by the same UC Santa Barbara team.

**Why it matters for doClima:** Enables heat stress indicators ([[wbgt]], extreme heat days, hot nights) at municipal scale — rare at this resolution for Brazil.
