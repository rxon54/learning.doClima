## Core Concepts

- **Estatuto da Cidade** (Lei 10.257/2001) — requires Plano Diretor for municipalities >20k inhabitants
- **Marco do Saneamento** (Lei 14.026/2020) — targets 99% water / 90% sewage by 2033
- **PPA / LDO / LOA** — if climate adaptation isn't in the PPA, it won't get funded
- ~50-100 municipalities have a municipal climate plan; quality varies wildly
- **Consórcios públicos** — inter-municipal partnerships for shared watershed/climate risks
