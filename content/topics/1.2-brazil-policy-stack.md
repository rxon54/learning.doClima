## Why it matters for doClima

doClima translates official Brazilian climate policy into usable intelligence. Knowing the stack makes you credible with government partners.

## Core Concepts

- **PNACC** — Plano Nacional de Adaptação à Mudança do Clima. 9 national objectives.
- **Guia de Adaptação** (MMA/GIZ, 3ª ed. 2025) — the 8-step municipal adaptation cycle
- **AdaptaBrasil/MCTI** — official risk assessment platform (~30 risk areas, 5,570 municipalities)
- **Brazil's NDC** under the Paris Agreement
- **Lei 12.187/2009** — Política Nacional sobre Mudança do Clima
- **Marco Legal do Clima (2024/2025)** — updated legal framework
