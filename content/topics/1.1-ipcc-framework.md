## Why it matters for doClima

Every AdaptaBrasil indicator, every Painel petal, maps back to this framework. You can't explain the product without it.

## Core Concepts

- **Risk = Hazard × Exposure × Vulnerability** (AR5)
- **AR6 update:** risk now includes response (maladaptation risk)
- Vulnerability = Sensitivity × (lack of) Adaptive Capacity
- **[[ssp-rcp]] scenario matrix:** SSPs = socioeconomic pathways, RCPs = radiative forcing. CMIP6 combines them.
- Key scenarios for Brazil: SSP2-4.5 (moderate) and SSP5-8.5 (fossil-fueled)
- Adaptation vs mitigation vs loss & damage
