## Core Concepts

- **Social Cost of Carbon** — economic damage per ton CO2. Estimates: $50-200/ton.
- **Loss and Damage** — UNFCCC framework for unavoidable climate impacts
- **[[tcfd]] / ISSB** — climate risk disclosure standards. Growing demand from companies and banks.
- **Adaptation economics** — $1.8T investment → $7.1T in net benefits (Global Commission on Adaptation)
- **BNDES Finem Clima** — main federal climate financing instrument
- **GCF / GEF / Adaptation Fund** — multilateral climate finance
