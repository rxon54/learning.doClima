## Core Concepts

- **[[gcm]]** — Global Climate Model. Physics-based, ~100-200 km resolution, ~40 models worldwide.
- **[[cmip6]]** — coordinated experiment feeding into IPCC AR6 and AdaptaBrasil.
- **[[ensemble]]** — multiple models on same scenario. Spread = structural uncertainty.
- **Scenarios vs predictions** — models answer "IF emissions follow X, THEN climate responds Y"
- **Uncertainty cascade:** emission scenario → model structure → natural variability
- **[[downscaling]] for Brazil:** Eta model (INPE), [[cordex]]-SA
