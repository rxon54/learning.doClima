## Core Concepts

- Watershed/basin delineation: water flows downhill — basin defines analysis unit, not municipality
- Runoff coefficient: how much rainfall becomes surface water (depends on soil, slope, vegetation, urbanisation)
- Why precipitation ≠ flood risk: 50mm on forested watershed vs 50mm on urban concrete are completely different
- Reservoir operation curves and water supply reliability
- [[idf-curves]] and return periods for flood engineering
