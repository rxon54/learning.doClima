## Core Concepts

- [[uhi]] — Urban Heat Island: cities 3-8°C warmer than surroundings
- Urban canyon effect: tall buildings trap heat, reduce ventilation
- Evapotranspiration cooling: trees cool via water evaporation — removing vegetation removes cooling
- Green infrastructure ROI: trees, green roofs, permeable surfaces reduce both heat and flood risk
- CHIRTS/ERA5 data may underestimate peak urban temperatures — UHI adjustment needed for capitals
