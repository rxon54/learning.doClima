## Why it matters for doClima

Before integrating S2ID/Atlas Digital data, you need to understand what the records represent and where the biases are.

## Core Concepts

- **[[cobrade]]** — Codificação Brasileira de Desastres. Key categories: 1.1 drought, 1.2 flood, 1.3 flash flood, 1.4 landslide, 1.5 storm.
- **S2ID / Atlas Digital** — national disaster DB since 1991. Records: type, date, affected municipalities, deaths, displaced, economic losses.
- **[[dec-ecp]]** — DEC = Decreto de Emergência (can manage with own resources). ECP = Estado de Calamidade Pública (resources overwhelmed). Both unlock federal aid.
- **Biases:**
  - Underreporting in municipalities without organized civil defense
  - Political incentive to declare emergencies (unlocks federal funds)
  - Death/economic loss estimates unreliable
  - Data quality improved significantly post-2012 (S2ID digitalization)
- Civil defense structure: CENAD → CEDEC → COMDEC. Not all 5,570 municipalities have a functional COMDEC.
