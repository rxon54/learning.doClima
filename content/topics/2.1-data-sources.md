## Why it matters for doClima

You make strategic data source decisions. Knowing what each source IS prevents architecture mistakes.

## Source Taxonomy

| Type | Examples | Strength | Limitation |
|------|----------|----------|------------|
| Station observations | INMET, ANA | Ground truth | Sparse coverage |
| Satellite estimates | [[chirps]], [[chirts]] | Full coverage | Uncertainty |
| Reanalysis | [[era5]] | Physical consistency | Not pure observations |
| Gridded products | CHIRPS, MERGE | Best of both | Inherit biases |
| GCM projections | [[cmip6]] | Future scenarios | Coarse, needs downscaling |

- **Bias correction** — raw model output has systematic errors; corrected using historical obs
- **[[downscaling]]** — coarse GCM → finer resolution. Dynamical (regional model) vs statistical.

## Practical Exercise
