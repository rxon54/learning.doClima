## Why it matters for doClima

You work with gridded climate data, municipal polygons, and land use rasters. Understanding the spatial layer prevents costly mistakes at the data pipeline level.

## Core Concepts

- **[[sirgas2000]]** (EPSG:4674) — Brazil's official datum
- **WGS84** (EPSG:4326) — GPS and international standard
- **Raster vs Vector** — climate data is raster; boundaries are vector
- **[[zonal-statistics]]** — how gridded data becomes a municipal indicator
- **Spatial resolution** — 0.05° ≈ 5.5 km; 0.25° ≈ 27 km
- **Map projections** — area, distance, shape can't all be preserved simultaneously
